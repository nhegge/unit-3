Data in LivingExpensesUnitedStatesMultivariateData.csv contains multivariate data on rent/income/employment and other general demographic information (median age/populations) for each State in the United States in 2024.

Data sources:

income: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/release/tables?eid=259515&rid=249

rent/population: https://www.rentdata.org/states/2024

formatted states list: https://github.com/jasonong/List-of-US-States/blob/master/states.csv

unemployment: https://www.bls.gov/news.release/pdf/srgune.pdf

median age: https://worldpopulationreview.com/state-rankings/median-age-by-state
